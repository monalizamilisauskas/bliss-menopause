Built by a woman who lives it

Bliss Menopause was created because we couldn't find an app that truly understood what perimenopause and menopause feel like from the inside — the brain fog, the sleepless nights, the moments of feeling like a stranger in your own body.

So we built it. Bliss is designed with empathy, grounded in science, and developed specifically for women who deserve better tools for this chapter of their lives.
